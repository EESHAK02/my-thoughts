# Between Versions

*Writing from the middle of things.*

---

This is a personal blog about the iterative nature of everything - ideas that seem brilliant until reality arrives, plans that unravel before they find their shape, and the quiet connections between things that seem entirely unrelated.

Essays here might come from building an app, reading a literary work, or simply noticing something. The subject, if anything, is the process - the space between the first draft and whatever comes next.

No advice. No conclusions. No claims. Just writing from that 'figuring out' space. 

---

## What you'll find here

- **Essays** - personal reflections on iteration, creativity, learning, and the messiness of getting anywhere
- **No niche** - technology and literature and life and building things all live here because they're all a part of something bigger
- **No preaching** - every piece is an observation, not a prescription

---

## The name

*Between Versions* is that place you live in when the old way has stopped working and the new way hasn't quite arrived. It's uncomfortable. It's also where everything interesting happens - it's where the stories are created!

---

## For the curious

This site is built with plain HTML and CSS. Just words and the simplest possible structure to hold them.
